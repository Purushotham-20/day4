Question 1

Recent Tragedic Event happened in Ahmedabad

Overview of the Incident

On June 12, 2025, Air India Flight AI171, a Boeing 787-8 Dreamliner, tragically crashed shortly after takeoff from Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport in Ahmedabad. The flight was en route to London Gatwick Airport and carried 242 individuals, including 230 passengers and 12 crew members. The crash resulted in the deaths of 241 people aboard and at least 38 individuals on the ground, including medical students residing in a hostel struck by the aircraft.

Investigation and Findings

Investigators have recovered both black boxes from the wreckage, which are being analyzed by Indian and international aviation authorities, including the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA), the Aircraft Accident Investigation Bureau (AAIB), and officials from Boeing and GE Aerospace. Preliminary findings suggest that the aircraft's emergency power system, the ram air turbine (RAT), was likely deployed prior to impact, indicating potential engine or hydraulic system malfunctions shortly after takeoff.

Personal Accounts and Aftermath

Among the victims was former Gujarat Chief Minister Vijay Rupani. The sole survivor, Viswash Kumar Ramesh, a British national seated near an emergency exit, recounted that the plane stalled midair before crashing into the hostel and igniting in a fireball. The incident has prompted widespread grief and calls for enhanced safety measures in aviation.
